About KUMI

More than just a beauty service

Kumi Salon was officially established on October 28, 2023, with a vision to create a welcoming space where beauty, confidence, and self-care come together. From the very beginning, our goal has been simple: to help every client look and feel their best.

We offer a wide variety of beauty services, carefully designed to meet different styles, needs, and personalities. Whether you're here for a refresh, a transformation, or regular self-care, our team is dedicated to delivering quality service with attention, creativity, and care.

Located at Mirqab Complex, First Floor Office 20, Doha, Qatar, Kumi Salon is proud to serve our community in a comfortable and relaxing environment.

What We Offer

Our Specialties

Hair Styling & Hair Treatments

We offer professional hair styling and advanced hair treatments, including Brazilian, Protein Collagen, and Nashi treatments, designed to restore, strengthen, and enhance the natural beauty of your hair.

Nail Care

We also offer professional nail care services, including gel polish, nail styling, and creative nail art, tailored to enhance and maintain beautiful, well-groomed nails.

Skincare and Facials

We offer different types of facial treatments tailored to various skin needs, including classic facials, BB Cream treatments, and advanced procedures such as Dermapen, using only Thalgo products to ensure safe, effective, and premium results.

Waxing & Soaking (Hyaluronic, Milk or Collagen)

We offer professional waxing and hair removal services designed to provide smooth, flawless skin while ensuring comfort and safety. We also offer soaking treatment that deeply nourishes & hydrates the skin using your choice of Milk, Hyaluronic, or Collagen infusion. Each option is designed to improve skin softness, moisture, and elasticity, leaving the body feeling smooth, refreshed, and well-nourished. We use a premium soaking product sourced from Japan.

Body Scrub

KUMI offers professional body scrub treatment designed to gently exfoliate the skin, remove dead skin cells, and promote natural skin renewal. Using carefully selected ingredients and soothing techniques, our body scrub service helps improve skin texture.
